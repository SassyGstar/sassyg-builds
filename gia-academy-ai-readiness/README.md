# GIA Academy — AI Readiness Assessment

A standalone, single-file self-assessment for **GIA Academy** (Governed. Intelligent. Accountable.) — part of GIA / ACE Advising. This is a separate project from the OGO Financial Services builds in this repository.

## What it does

A 12-question, self-scored readiness profile that helps experienced professionals gauge where they stand across:

- AI knowledge and application
- Strategy and business value
- Governance, risk, and human oversight
- Confidence, communication, and career direction

Answers are scored 1–5 and mapped to one of five readiness levels — **AI Curious → AI Aware → AI Capable → AI Governed → AI Leadership Ready** — each with a personalized summary and a recommended next step.

## How to use

It's a single HTML file — everything (styles, logo, and scoring logic) is self-contained. Open `index.html` in any browser, or view it live once published:

🌐 [sassygstar.github.io/sassyg-builds/gia-academy-ai-readiness/](https://sassygstar.github.io/sassyg-builds/gia-academy-ai-readiness/)

All scoring runs in the browser; no data is collected or sent anywhere.

## Note

This is an educational readiness profile, not a professional certification, legal determination, or guarantee of employment or business results.
